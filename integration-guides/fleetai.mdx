---
title: Netstar Australia (FleetAI)
---

To connect your Netstar telematics data with PathStack, you will need the following access credentials:
* API Key
* Client Name

To obtain these credentials please contact your Netstar account representative and request API access to your account data.

To get the Client Name, go to https://ubi-api.netstaraus.com.au/swagger/index.html and open the /vehicle/vehicles API and put in your API key.
Execute the request and then copy the Client Name from the vehicles.

<Tip>If you don't have your Netstar representative's details you can contact their team on 1800 325 052 or at customer.support@netstaraus.com.au.</Tip>
