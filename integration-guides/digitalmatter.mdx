---
title: Digital Matter
---

Configuring Digital Matter devices entails setting up the binary-encoded data stream target in the Telematics Guru platform.
This configuration allows the platform to interpret data accurately. 
Please contact Nuonic support for more information on integrating your Digital Matter device to Pathstack.
