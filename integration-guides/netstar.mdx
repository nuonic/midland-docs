To connect your Teletrac Navman telematics data with PathStack, you will need the following access credentials:
* Username
* Password
* API URL Prefix

To obtain these credentials please contact your Netstar account representative and request API access to your account data.

<Tip>If you don't have your Netstar representative's details you can contact their team on 1800 325 052 or at customer.support@netstaraus.com.au.</Tip>
