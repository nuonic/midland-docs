title: Radius Telematics
---

To connect your Radius Telematics data with PathStack, you will need the following access credentials:
* API Key
* Client ID

To obtain these credentials please contact your Radius Telematics account representative and request API access to your account data.

<Tip>If you don't have your Radius representative's details you can contact their team on 0800 056 4432 or at info@radiustelematics.com.</Tip>
