To connect your Verizon Connect telematics data with PathStack, you will need the following access credentials:
* Username
* Password
* App ID

<Note>For Verizon FTC customers, our team can assist with this process. Please contact us on 1300 822 370 for more details.</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a new read-only Reveal user">
    Create a new user in Verizon's Reveal platform by following [this help centre guide](https://reveal-help.verizonconnect.com/hc/en-au/articles/360010706839-Creating-and-editing-users). We advise assigning this user the Analyst role and providing read-only permissions to account data. The Username and Password credentials required to connect to PathStack are those assigned to this user.
    <img src="/images/integration-guides/vzc-1.png" />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Register a new app on Verizon's developer portal">
    To obtain the App ID credential, you need to register a new app on Verizon's developer portal for your Verizon account. Please see [this guide](https://reveal-help.verizonconnect.com/hc/en-au/articles/10933751995539-Developer-portal-overview) for instructions on how to do this.
    <img src="/images/integration-guides/vzc-2.png" />
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>For more help obtaining these credentials, please contact your Verizon Customer Success Manager or their office via 1800 039 642 or supportaus@verizonconnect.com</Tip>
