To connect your Fleetsu telematics device integration with PathStack, you will need to be provided with a username and 
password for accessing the API. The subsequent procedures will guide you through the process.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log into your Fleet Management">
    The username and password that you use to log into your Fleetsu fleet management account are the same credentials 
    that you will use for accessing the API.

  </Step>
</Steps>
